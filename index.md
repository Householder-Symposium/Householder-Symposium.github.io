### June 8 &ndash; June 13, 2025; Cornell University &mdash; Ithaca, NY USA

![Cornell University campus with Cayuga lake in the background](images/UP_2016_1413_089_select.jpg)

The XXII Householder Symposium on Numerical Linear Algebra will be held June 8 - June 13, 2025 at the [Statler Hotel](https://statlerhotel.cornell.edu) on [Cornell University](https://www.cornell.edu/)'s campus in Ithaca, NY. The local organizing committee is [Anil Damle](mailto:damle@cornell.edu) (chair), [Alex Townsend](mailto:ajt253@cornell.edu), and [David Bindel](mailto:bindel@cornell.edu). Applications to attend are now closed; we anticipate extending invitations to attend by the end of February 2025. Nominations for the Alston S. Householder Prize for the best dissertation in numerical linear algebra are now open and are due by February 16, 2025. Instructions to submit a nomination are available [here](/prize/).
